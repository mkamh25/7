SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 775:

Narrated Um Salama:
that once the Prophet was in her house, and an effeminate man was there too. The effeminate man
said to 'Abdullah, (Um Salama's brother) "0 'Abdullah! If Ta'if should be conquered tomorrow, I recommend
you the daughter of Ghailan, for she is so fat that she has four curves in the front (of her
belly) and eight at the back." So the Prophet said (to his wives) "These effeminate (men) should not
enter upon you (your houses).
