SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 488:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
I was serving Abu 'Ubaida, Abu Talha and Ubai bin Ka'b with a drink prepared from ripe and unripe
dates. Then somebody came to them and said, "Alcoholic drinks have been prohibited." (On
hearing that) Abu Talha said, "Get up. O Anas, and pour (throw) it out! So I poured (threw) it out.
