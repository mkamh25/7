SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 114:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
The Prophet said, "Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day should not hurt (trouble) his neighbor.
And I advise you to take care of the women, for they are created from a rib and the most
crooked portion of the rib is its upper part; if you try to straighten it, it will break, and if you leave it,
it will remain crooked, so I urge you to take care of the women."
