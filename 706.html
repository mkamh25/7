SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 706:

Narrated 'Aisha and 'Abdullah bin 'Abbas:
When the disease of Allah's Apostle got aggravated, he covered his face with a Khamisa, but when
he became short of breath, he would remove it from his face and say, "It is like that! May Allah curse
the Jews Christians because they took the graves of their prophets as places of worship." By that he
warned his follower of imitating them, by doing that which they did.
