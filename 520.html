SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 520:

Narrated An-Nazzal bin Sabra:
'Ali offered the Zuhr prayer and then sat down in the wide courtyard (of the Mosque) of Kufa in
order to deal with the affairs of the people till the 'Asr prayer became due. Then water was brought
to him and he drank of it, washed his face, hands, head and feet. Then he stood up and drank the re-
maining water while he was standing. and said, "Some people dislike to drink water while standing
thought the Prophet did as I have just done."
