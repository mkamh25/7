SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 181:

Narrated Al-Awza:
I asked Az-Zuhri, "Which of the wives of the Prophet sought refuge with Allah from him?" He said
"I was told by 'Ursa that 'Aisha said, 'When the daughter of Al-Jaun was brought to Allah's Apostle (as
his bride) and he went near her, she said, "I seek refuge with Allah from you." He said, "You have
sought refuge with The Great; return to your family."
