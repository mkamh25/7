SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 70, Number 579:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Whenever Allah's Apostle paid a visit to a patient, or a patient was brought to him, he used to invoke
Allah, saying, "Take away the disease, O the Lord of the people! Cure him as You are the One
Who cures. There is no cure but Yours, a cure that leaves no disease."
