SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 108:

Narrated Nafi':
Abdullah bin 'Umar said, "Allah's Apostle said, 'Accept the marriage invitation if you are invited to
it.' " Ibn 'Umar used to accept the invitation whether to a wedding banquet or to any other party,
even when he was fasting.
