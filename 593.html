SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 593:

Narrated 'Ursa:
Aisha used to recommend At-Talbina for the sick and for such a person as grieved over a dead
person. She used to say, "I heard Allah's Apostle saying, 'At-Talbina gives rest to the heart of the patient
and makes it active and relieves some of his sorrow and grief.' "
