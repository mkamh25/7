SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 316:

Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:
The Prophet ate of the meat of a shoulder (by cutting the meat with his teeth), and then got up and
offered the prayer without performing the ablution anew. Narrated Ibn 'Abbas: The Prophet took out
a bone with meat on it from a cooking pot and ate of it, and then offered the prayer without performing
ablution anew.
