SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 144:

Narrated 'Aisha:
that during his fatal ailment, Allah's Apostle, used to ask his wives, "Where shall I stay tomorrow?
Where shall I stay tomorrow?" He was looking forward to Aisha's turn. So all his wives allowed him
to stay where he wished, and he stayed at 'Aisha's house till he died there. 'Aisha added: He died on
the day of my usual turn at my house. Allah took him unto Him while his head was between my
chest and my neck and his saliva was mixed with my saliva.
