SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 416:

Narrates 'Abdullah bin Mughaffal:
While we were besieging the castle of Khaibar, Somebody threw a skin full of fat and I went ahead
to take it, but on looking behind, I saw the Prophet and I felt shy in his presence (and did not take it).
