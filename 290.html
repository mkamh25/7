SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 290:

Narrated Wahb bin Kaisan Abi Nu'aim:
A meal was brought to Allah's Apostle while his step-son, 'Umar bin Abi Salama was with him. Allah's
Apostle said to him, "Mention the Name of Allah and eat of the dish what is nearer to you."
