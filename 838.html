SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 838:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Allah's Apostle returned from a journey when I had placed a curtain of mine having pictures over
(the door of) a chamber of mine. When Allah's Apostle saw it, he tore it and said, "The people who
will receive the severest punishment on the Day of Resurrection will be those who try to make the
like of Allah's creations." So we turned it (i.e., the curtain) into one or two cushions.
