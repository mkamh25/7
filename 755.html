SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 755:

Narrated 'Abdullah:
Allah's Apostle wore a gold or silver .. ring and placed its stone towards the palm of his hand. The
people also started wearing gold rings like it, but when the Prophet saw them wearing such rings, he
threw away that golden ring and then wore a silver ring.
