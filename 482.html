SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 482:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
On the night Allah's Apostle was taken on a night journey (Miraj) two cups, one containing wine
and the other milk, were presented to him at Jerusalem. He looked at it and took the cup of milk.
Gabriel said, "Praise be to Allah Who guided you to Al-Fitra (the right path); if you had taken (the
cup of) wine, your nation would have gone astray."
