SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 616:

Narrated Um Oais:
that she took to Allah's Apostle one of her sons whose palate and tonsils she had pressed to treat a
throat trouble. The Prophet said, "Be afraid of Allah! Why do you pain your children by having their
tonsils pressed like that? Use the Ud Al-Hindi (a certain Indian incense) for it cures seven diseases,
one of which is pleurisy."
