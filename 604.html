SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 604:

Narrated Ka'b bin Ujrah:
The Prophet came to me during the period of Al-Hudaibiya, while I was lighting fire underneath a
cooking pot and lice were falling down my head. He said, "Do your lice hurt your?" I said, "Yes." He
said, "Shave your head and fast for three days or feed six poor persons or slaughter a sheep as a sacrifice:"
