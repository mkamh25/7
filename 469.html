SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 68, Number 469:

Narrated Jundab bin Sufyan Al-Bajali:
I witnessed the Prophet on the Day of Nahr. He said, "Whoever slaughtered the sacrifice before offering
the 'Id prayer, should slaughter another sacrifice in its place; and whoever has not slaughtered
their sacrifice yet, should slaughter now."
