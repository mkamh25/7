SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 822:

Narrated 'Alqama:
'Abdullah cursed those women who practiced tattooing and those who removed hair from their
faces and those who created spaces between their teeth artificially to look beautiful, such ladies as
changed what Allah has created. Um Ya'qub said, "What is that?" 'Abdullah said, "Why should I not
curse those who were cursed by Allah's Apostle and are referred to in Allah's Book?" She said to him
"By Allah, I have read the whole Qur'an but I have not found such a thing. 'Abdullah said, "By Allah,
if you had read it (carefully) you would have found it. (Allah says:) 'And what the Apostle gives you
take it and what he forbids you abstain (from it).' (59.7)
