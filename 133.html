SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 133:

Narrated 'Aisha:
An Ansari woman gave her daughter in marriage and the hair of the latter started falling out. The
Ansari women came to the Prophet and mentioned that to him and said, "Her (my daughter's) husband
suggested that I should let her wear false hair." The Prophet said, "No, (don't do that) for Allah
sends His curses upon such ladies who lengthen their hair artificially."
