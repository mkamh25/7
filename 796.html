SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 796:

Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar :
I heard 'Umar saying, "Whoever braids his hair should shave it (on finishing lhram). You'd better
not do, something like Talbid." Ibn Umar used to say: "I saw Allah's Apostle with his hair stuck together
with gum."
