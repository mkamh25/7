SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 194:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
The Prophet said, "Allah has forgiven my followers the evil thoughts that occur to their minds, as
long as such thoughts are not put into action or uttered." And Qatada said, "If someone divorces his
wife just in his mind, such an unuttered divorce has no effect.:
