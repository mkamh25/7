SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 415:

Narrated 'Aisha:
A group of people said to the Prophet, "Some people bring us meat and we do not know whether
they have mentioned Allah's Name or not on slaughtering the animal." He said, "Mention Allah's
Name on it and eat." Those people had embraced Islam recently.
