SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 708:

Narrated Aisha:
Allah's Apostle offered prayer while he was wearing a Khamisa of his that had printed marks. He
looked at its marks and when he finished prayer, he said, "Take this Khamisa of mine to Abu Jahm,
for it has just now diverted my attention from my prayer, and bring to me the Anbijania (a plain
thick sheet) of Abu Jahm bin Hudhaifa bin Ghanim who belonged to Bani Adi bin Ka'b."
