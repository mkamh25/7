SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 693:

Narrated 'Uqba bin 'Amir:
A silken Farruj was presented to Allah's Apostle and he put it on and offered the prayer in it. When
he finished the prayer, he took it off violently as if he disliked it and said, "This (garment) does not
befit those who fear Allah!"
