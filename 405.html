SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 405:

Narrated Salama bin Al-Aqwa':
In the evening of the day of the conquest of Khaibar, the army made fires (for cooking). The
Prophet said, "For what have you made these fires?" They said, "For cooking the meat of domestic
donkeys." He said, "Throw away what is in the cooking pots and break the pots." A man from the
people got up and said, "Shall we throw the contents of the cooking pots and then wash the pots (instead
of breaking them)?" The Prophet said, "Yes, you can do either'
