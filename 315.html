SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 315:

Narrated Sahl bin Sad:
We used to be happy on Fridays, for there was an old lady who used to pull out the roots of Silq
and put it in a cooking pot with some barley. When we had finished the prayer, we would visit her
and she would present that dish before us. So we used to be happy on Fridays because of that, and we
never used to take our meals or have a mid-day nap except after the Friday prayer. By Allah, that
meal contained no fat.
