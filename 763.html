SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 763:

Narrated Anas:
The Prophet got a ring made for himself and said, "I have got a ring made (for myself) and engraved
a certain engraving on it so none of you should get such an engraving on his ring." I saw the
glitter of the ring on his little finger.
