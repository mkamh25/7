Narrated Abu Zur'a:

a gnat.' "Abu Huraira then asked for a water container and washed his arms up to his armpits. I said,
"0 Abu i Huraira! Is this something you have heard I from Allah's Apostle?" He said, "The limit for
ablution is up to the place where the ornaments will reach on the Day of Resurrection.'
