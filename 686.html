SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 686:

Narrated Ibn 'Umar:
A man asked, "O Allah s Apostle What kind of clothes should a Muhrim wear?" The Prophet, said,
"A Muhrim should not wear a shirt, trousers a hooded cloak, or Khuffs (leather socks covering the
ankles) unless he cannot get sandals, in which case he should cut the part (of the Khuff) that covers
the ankles."
