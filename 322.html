SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 322:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
Once the Prophet distributed dates among his companions and gave each one seven dates. He gave
me seven dates too, one of which was dry and hard, but none of the other dates was more liked by
me than that one, for it prolonged my chewing it.
