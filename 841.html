SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 841:

Narrated Abu Talha:
Allah's Apostle said, "Angels (of mercy) do not enter a house where there are pictures.'" The subnarrator
Busr added: "Then Zaid fell ill and we paid him a visit. Behold! There was, hanging at his
door, a curtain decorated with a picture. I said to 'Ubaidullah Al-Khaulani, the step son of Maimuna,
the wife of the Prophet , "Didn't Zaid tell us about the picture the day before yesterday?" 'Ubaidullah
said, "Didn't you hear him saying: 'except a design in a garment'?"
