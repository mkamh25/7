SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 495:

Narrated Sahl:
Abu Usaid As-Sa'idi came and invited Allah's Apostle on the occasion of his wedding. His wife who
was the bride, was serving them. Do you know what drink she prepared for Allah's Apostle ? She had
soaked some dates in water in a Tur overnight.
