SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 522:

Narrated Um Al-Fadl:
(daughter of Al-Harith) that she sent a bowl of milk to the Prophet while he was standing (at 'Arafat)
in the afternoon of the Day of 'Arafat. He took it in his hands and drank it. Narrated Abu Nadr:
The Prophet was on the back of his camel.
