SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 112:

Narrated Sahl bin Sad:
Abu Usaid As-Sa'idi invited the Prophet to his wedding party and his wife served him on that day,
and she was the bride. She said (or Sahl said), "Do you know what she soaked for Allah's Apostle? She
soaked some dates for him (in water) in a drinking bowl overnight."
