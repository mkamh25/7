SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 519:

Narrated An-Nazzal:
All came to the gate of the courtyard (of the Mosque) and drank (water) while he was standing
and said, "Some people dislike to drink while standing, but I saw the Prophet doing (drinking water)
as you have seen me doing now."
