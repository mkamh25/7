SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 639:

Narrated 'Aisha:
The Prophet used to treat some of his wives by passing his right hand over the place of ailment
and used to say, "O Allah, the Lord of the people! Remove the trouble and heal the patient, for You
are the Healer. No healing is of any avail but Yours; healing that will leave behind no ailment."
