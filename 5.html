SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 5:

Narrated 'Ata:
We presented ourselves along with Ibn 'Abbas at the funeral procession of Maimuna at a place
called Sarif. Ibn 'Abbas said, "This is the wife of the Prophet so when you lift her bier, do not Jerk it or
shake it much, but walk smoothly because the Prophet had nine wives and he used to observe the
night turns with eight of them, and for one of them there was no night turn."
