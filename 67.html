SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 67:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
The Prophet said, "A matron should not be given in marriage except after consulting her; and a
virgin should not be given in marriage except after her permission." The people asked, "O Allah's
Apostle! How can we know her permission?" He said, "Her silence (indicates her permission)."
