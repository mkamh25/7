SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 68, Number 459:

Narrated Nafi':
'Abdullah (bin 'Umar) used to slaughter his sacrifice at the slaughtering place (i.e the slaughtering
place of the Prophet ) . Ibn 'Umar said, "Allah's Apostle used to slaughter (camels and sheep, etc.,) as
sacrifices at the Musalla."
