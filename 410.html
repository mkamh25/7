SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 410:

Narrated 'Abdullah
that Ka'b had a slave girl who used to graze his sheep on a small mountain, called "Sl'a", situated
near the market. Once a sheep was dying, so she broke a stone and slaughtered it with it. When they
mentioned that to the Prophet, he, permitted them to eat it.
