SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 314:

Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:
My aunt presented (roasted) mastigures, Iqt and milk to the Prophet . The mastigures were put on
his dining sheet, and if it was unlawful to eat, it would not have been put there. The Prophet drank
the milk and ate the Iqt only.
