SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 289:

Narrated 'Umar bin Al Salama:
Who was the son of Um Salama, the wife of the Prophet:
Once I ate a meal with Allah's Apostle and I was eating from all sides of the dish. So Allah's Apostle
said to me, "Eat of the dish what is nearer to you."
