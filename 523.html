SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 523:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
Milk mixed with water was brought to Allah's Apostle while a bedouin was on his right and Abu
Bakr was on his left. He drank (of it) and then gave (it) to the bedouin and said, 'The right" "The right
(first)."
