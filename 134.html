SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 134:

Narrated Aisha:
regarding the Verse: 'If a wife fears cruelty or desertion on her husband's part ...') (4.128) It concerns
the woman whose husband does not want to keep her with him any longer, but wants to divorce
her and marry some other lady, so she says to him: 'Keep me and do not divorce me, and then
marry another woman, and you may neither spend on me, nor sleep with me.' This is indicated by
the Statement of Allah: 'There is no blame on them if they arrange an amicable settlement between
them both, and (such) settlement is better." (4.128)
