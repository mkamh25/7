SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 225:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
A man came to the Prophet and said, "O Allah's Apostle! A black child has been born for me." The
Prophet asked him, "Have you got camels?" The man said, "Yes." The Prophet asked him, "What color
are they?" The man replied, "Red." The Prophet said, "Is there a grey one among them?' The man
replied, "Yes." The Prophet said, "Whence comes that?" He said, "May be it is because of heredity." The
Prophet said, "May be your latest son has this color because of heredity."
