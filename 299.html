SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 299:

Narrated Anas:
The Prophet halted to consummate his marriage with Safiyya. I invited the Muslims to his wedding
banquet. He ordered that leather dining sheets be spread. Then dates, dried yoghurt and butter were
put on those sheets. Anas added: The Prophet consummated his marriage with Safiyya (during a
journey) whereupon Hais (sweet dish) was served on a leather dining sheet.
