SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 667:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
Allah's Apostle said, "No 'Adha." Abu Huraira also said: The Prophet said, "The cattle suffering from
a disease should not be mixed up with healthy cattle (or said "Do not put a patient with a healthy
person as a precaution.") Abu Huraira also said: Allah's Apostle said, "No 'Adha." A bedouin got up
and said, "Don't you see how camels on the sand look like deer but when a mangy camel mixes with
them, they all get infected with mange?" On that the Prophet said, "Then who conveyed the (mange)
disease to the first camel?"
