SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 14:

Narrated 'Aisha :
I said, "O Allah's Apostle! Suppose you landed in a valley where there is a tree of which something
has been eaten and then you found trees of which nothing has been eaten, of which tree would you
let your camel graze?" He said, "(I will let my camel graze) of the one of which nothing has been
eaten before." (The sub-narrator added: 'Aisha meant that Allah's Apostle had not married a virgin
besides herself .)
