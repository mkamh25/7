SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 611:

Narrated Um Qais:
I went to Allah's Apostle along with a a son of mine whose palate and tonsils I had pressed with
my finger as a treatment for a (throat and tonsil) disease. The Prophet said, "Why do you pain your
children by pressing their throats! Use Ud Al-Hindi (certain Indian incense) for it cures seven diseases,
one of which is pleurisy. It is used as a snuff for treating throat and tonsil disease and it is inserted
into one side of the mouth of one suffering from pleurisy."
