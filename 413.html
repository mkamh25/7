SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 413:

Narrated Mu'adh bin Sad or Sad bin Mu'adh:
A slave girl belonging to Ka'b used to graze some sheep at Sl'a (mountain). Once one of her sheep
was dying. She reached it (before it died) and slaughtered it with a stone. The Prophet was asked, and
he said, "Eat it."
