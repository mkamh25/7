SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 644:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Whenever Allah's Apostle went to bed, he used to recite Surat-al-Ikhlas, Surat-al-Falaq and Suratan-
Nas and then blow on his palms and pass them over his face and those parts of his body that his
hands could reach. And when he fell ill, he used to order me to do like that for him.
