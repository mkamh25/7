SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 68, Number 462:

Narrated 'Uqba bin 'Amir:
that the Prophet gave him some sheep to distribute among his companions to slaughter as sacrifices
('Id--al--Adha). A kid was left and he told the Prophet of that whereupon he said to him,
"Slaughter it as a sacrifice (on your behalf)."
