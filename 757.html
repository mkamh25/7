SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 757:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
that he saw a silver ring on the hand of Allah's Apostle for one day only. Then the people had silver
rings made for themselves and wore it. On that, Allah's Apostle threw away their rings as well.
(For the details of this Hadith, see Fateh-Al-Bari, Vol. 12, page 438).
