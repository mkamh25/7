SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 85:

Narrated Anas:
The Prophet saw the traces of Sufra (yellow perfume) on Abdur-Rahman bin 'Auf and said, "What
is this?" 'Abdur-Rahman, said, "I have married a woman and have paid gold equal to the weight of a
datestone (as her Mahr). The Prophet said to him, "May Allah bless you: Offer a wedding banquet
even with one sheep."
