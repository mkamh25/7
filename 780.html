SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 780:

Narrated Nafi':
Ibn Umar said, The Prophet said, 'Do the opposite of what the pagans do. Keep the beards and cut
the moustaches short.' Whenever Ibn 'Umar performed the Hajj or 'Umra, he used to hold his beard
with his hand and cut whatever moustaches. Ibn Umar used to cut his moustache so short that the
whiteness of his skin (above the upper lip) was visible, and he used to cut (the hair) between his
moustaches and his beard.
