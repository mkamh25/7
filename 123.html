SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 123:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
Allah's Apostle said, "It is not lawful for a lady to fast (Nawafil) without the permission of her husband
when he is at home; and she should not allow anyone to enter his house except with his permission;
and if she spends of his wealth (on charitable purposes) without being ordered by him, he
will get half of the reward."
