SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 772:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
I was with Allah's Apostle in one of the Markets of Medina. He left (the market) and so did I. Then
he asked thrice, "Where is the small (child)?" Then he said, "Call Al-Hasan bin 'Ali." So Al-Hasan bin
'Ali got up and started walking with a necklace (of beads) around his neck. The Prophet stretched his
hand out like this, and Al-Hasan did the same. The Prophet embraced him and said, "0 Allah! l love
him, so please love him and love those who love him." Since Allah's Apostle said that. nothing has
been dearer to me than Al-Hasan.
