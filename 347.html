SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 347:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
A tailor invited the Prophet to a meal which he had prepared, and I went along with the Prophet .
The tailor presented barley bread and soup containing gourd and cured meat. I saw the Prophet
picking the pieces of gourd from around the dish, and since then I have kept on liking gourd.
