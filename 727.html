Volume 7, Book 72, Number 727:

Narrated Al-Bara:
The Prophet was given a silk garment as a gift and we started touching it with our hands and admiring
it. On that the Prophet said, "Do you wonder at this?" We said, "Yes." He said, "The handkerchiefs
of Sad bin Mu'adh in Paradise are better than this "
