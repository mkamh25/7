SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 71, Number 587:

Narrated Jabir bin Abdullah:
I heard the Prophet saying, "If there is any healing in your medicines, then it is in cupping, a gulp
of honey or branding with fire (cauterization) that suits the ailment, but I don't like to be (cauterized)
branded with fire."
