SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 70, Number 565:

Narrated 'Abdullah:
I visited the Prophet during his illness and touched him while he was having a fever. I said to him,
"You have a high fever; is it because you will get a double reward?" He said, "Yes. No Muslim is afflicted
with any harm, but that his sins will be annulled as the leave of a tree fall down."
