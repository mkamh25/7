SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 709:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
The Prophet had forbidden: (A) the Mulamasa and Munabadha (bargains), (B) the offering of two
prayers, one after the morning compulsory prayer till the sun rises, and the others, after the 'Asr
prayer till the sun sets (C) He also forbade that one should sit wearing one garment, nothing of
which covers his private parts (D) and prevent them from exposure to the sky; (E) he also forbade
Ishtimal-as-Samma'.
