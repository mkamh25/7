SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 227:

Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:
Hilal bin Umaiyya accused his wife of illegal sexual intercourse and came to the Prophet to bear
witness (against her), (taking the oath of Lian). The Prophet was saying, "Allah knows that either of
you is a liar. Will anyone of you repent (to Allah)?" Then the lady got up and gave her witness.
