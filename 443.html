SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 67, Number 443:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
Once we provoked a rabbit at Marr-az-Zahran. The people chased it till they got tired. Then I
caught It and brought it to Abu Talha, who slaughtered it and then sent both its pelvic pieces (or
legs) to the Prophet, and the Prophet accepted the present.
