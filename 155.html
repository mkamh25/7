SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 155:

Narrated Aisha:
That Allah's Apostle said to her, "I you are pleased with me or angry with me." I said, "Whence do
you know that?" He said, "When you are pleased with me, you say, 'No, by the Lord of Muhammad,'
but when you are angry with me, then you say, 'No, by the Lord of Abraham.' " Thereupon I said, "Yes
(you are right), but by Allah, O Allah's Apostle, I leave nothing but your name."
