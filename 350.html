SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 350:

Narrated Anas bin Malik:
A tailor invited Allah's Apostle to a meal which he had prepared. I went with Allah's Apostle to that
meal, and the tailor served the Prophet with barley bread and soup of gourd and cured meat. I saw
Allah's Apostle picking the pieces of gourd from around the dish, and since then I have kept on liking
gourd.
