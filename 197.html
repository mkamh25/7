SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 197:

Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:
The wife of Thabit bin Qais came to the Prophet and said, "O Allah's Apostle! I do not blame Thabit
for defects in his character or his religion, but I, being a Muslim, dislike to behave in un-Islamic
manner (if I remain with him)." On that Allah's Apostle said (to her), "Will you give back the garden
which your husband has given you (as Mahr)?" She said, "Yes." Then the Prophet said to Thabit, "O
Thabit! Accept your garden, and divorce her once."
