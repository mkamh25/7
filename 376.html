SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 66, Number 376:

Narrated Abu Musa:
A son was born to me and I took him to the Prophet who named him Ibrahim, did Tahnik for him
with a date, invoked Allah to bless him and returned him to me. (The narrator added: That was Abu
Musa's eldest son.)
