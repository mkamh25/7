SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 68, Number 455:

Narrated 'Uqba bin 'Amir Al-Juhani:
that the Prophet distributed among his companions some animals for sacrifice (to be slaughtered
on 'Id-al-Adha). 'Uqba's share was a Jadha'a (a six month old goat). 'Uqba said, "O Allah's Apostle! I
get in my share of Jadha'a (a six month old ram)." The Prophet said, "Slaughter it as a sacrifice."
