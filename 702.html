SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 72, Number 702:

Narrated Abu Huraira:
I heard Allah's Apostle saying "From among my followers, a group (o 70,000) will enter Paradise
without being asked for their accounts, Their faces will be shining like the moon." 'Ukasha bin
Muhsin Al-Asadi got up, lifting his covering sheet and said, "O Allah's Apostle Invoke Allah for me
that He may include me with them." The Prophet said! "O Allah! Make him from them." Then another
man from Al-Ansar got up and said, "O Allah's Apostle! Invoke Allah for me that He may include me
with them." On that Allah's Apostle said, "'Ukasha has anticipated you."
