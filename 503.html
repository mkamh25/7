SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 69, Number 503:

Narrated Abu Al-Juwairiyya:
I asked Ibn 'Abbas about Al-Badhaq. He said, "Muhammad prohibited alcoholic drinks before It
was called Al-Badhaq (by saying), 'Any drink that intoxicates is unlawful.' I said, 'What about good
lawful drinks?' He said,'Apart from what is lawful and good, all other things are unlawful and not
good (unclean Al-Khabith).
