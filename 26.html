SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 26:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Allah's Apostle entered upon Dubaa bint Az-Zubair and said to her, "Do you have a desire to perform
the Hajj?" She replied, "By Allah, I feel sick." He said to her, "Intend to perform Hajj and stipulate
something by saying, 'O Allah, I will finish my Ihram at any place where You stop me (i.e. I am unable
to go further)." She was the wife of Al-Miqdad bin Al-Aswad.
