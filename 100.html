SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 62, Number 100:

Narrated Thabit:
The marriage of Zainab bint Jahash was mentioned in the presence of Anas and he said, "I did not
see the Prophet giving a better banquet on marrying any of his wives than the one he gave on marrying
Zainab. He then gave a banquet with one sheep."
