SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 65, Number 333:

Narrated 'Amr bin Umaiyay Ad-Damri:
I saw Allah's Apostle cutting part of the shoulder of mutton with a knife. He ate of it and then was
called for prayer whereupon he got up and put down the knife and offered the prayer without performing
new ablution.
