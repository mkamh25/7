SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 68, Number 466:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Allah's Apostle entered upon me at Sarif while I was weeping (because I was afraid that I would
not be able to perform the ,Hajj). He said, "What is wrong with you? Have you got your period?" I
replied, "Yes." He said, "This is a matter Allah has decreed for all the daughters of Adam, so perform
the ceremonies of the Hajj as the pilgrims do, but do not perform the Tawaf around the Ka'ba." Allah's
Apostle slaughtered some cows as sacrifices on behalf of his wives.
