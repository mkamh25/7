SAHIH BUKHARI

Volume 7, Book 63, Number 238:

Narrated 'Aisha:
Rifa'a Al-Qurazi married a lady and then divorced her whereupon she married another man. She
came to the Prophet and said that her new husband did not approach her, and that he was completely
impotent. The Prophet said (to her), "No (you cannot remarry your first husband) till you taste
the second husband and he tastes you (i.e. till he consummates his marriage with you)."
